Hello!!!
I am so glad that you were able to stop by my new website. Launched recently in 2018, as a community page for toy collectors,
and anyone who wishes to reminisce on their childhood,this website still has room to grow and I am working to improve it further
for everyone and make it as enjoyable and interactive as possible. I could not have gotten this website started without the help of
my professor from UC Davis's Web Development program.

As a child, I was always encouraged to play and use my imagination as much as possible. Every day there was a new story to be made up,
places to travel to, and toys to give life to. In my early teens, my mom began collecting, repairing, and selling vintage dolls from around
the world. There was always a surprising amount of history that came with each and every one of her findings. Over the years we have developed
a broad knowledge of dolls, and all of them leave some sort of impression in our minds. With her help, I was able create the "History" page of
this website so that other people can learn more about where their toys came from and who created them.

What is the story behind my name "Sandarin-CY01" you ask? "Sandarin" is a lighthearted play on my name, Sandra. This nickname was given to
me by a very good friend. I enjoy how it just rolls off your tongue. C just stands for my last name and the "Y01" has no special meaning behind
it other than to create a more unique name for myself online.
